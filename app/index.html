HOW YOU CAN USE THIS REPORT

Through this sustainability report we wish to contribute to increased knowledge about the value of reuse, and keeping products alive, as well as increased understanding about the importance of a reduced resource appetite. By providing regular reports you will see the change YOU contribute to in numbers, free to communicate in accordance with your preferences.

With the help of the data and facts we gather about how your company contributes to a better world, we hope to be able to develop more collaborations for improved sustainability.

Our sustainability reports, and the underlying data and calculations, are in constant development. Right now we are collaborating with the LCA experts at Miljögiraff to develop a tool that can generate more representative sustainability data for all products we handle for you. The work is in development and is expected to be completed in early 2023.

- The sustainability team at GIAB Nordic AB

SUSTAINABLE CAPITALISM

At GIAB we strive towards developing the concept of sustainable capitalism both within our own business and for our cooperation partners.

Sustainable business practices is the future, and we can help you and your company achieve the desired impact of your efforts towards your sustainability journey.

The consulting team at GIAB can help your company by developing plans of action customized for your company and your long- term ambitions. We can also aid in quantifying and measuring the impact of carbon emissions, certifications as well as the development of test pilots for reuse hubs or with education activities within the field of sustainable business development.

The possibilities are huge and together we can make a real difference!
